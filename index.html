Fighting for Birdie

Florida's Premier Golf Simulator Bar

Have you ever wanted to be able to play golf year around, but live in Florida where it's storming 80% of the time or too hot to even breathe? Well Fighting for Birdie is here for you. With 6 simulators to use (4 for tee times and 2 to use as a range) there is always a way for you to get that round of 18 in or a quick warmup session before your round.

Ever wanted to play TPC Sawgrass or Torrey Pines? With our TRACKMAN simulators, YOU CAN!!! With over 600 of the worlds best courses available to play, you can enjoy these elite courses for a fraction of the cost from the comfort of our building.
